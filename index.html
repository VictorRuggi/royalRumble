This simulator does not take into account surprise entrants like legends, however, entrants that are injured, whether they are legit or kayfabe injuries (aka, storyline) and who are still on the active WWE roster can make a surprise appearance in the match. Just like in the real match, a new wrestler enters at every 90 seconds until all 30 wrestlers have entered the match, and unlike the WWE, I mean 90 seconds and not 2 or 3 minutes at times :). The last wrestler standing after all 30 wrestlers have entered will be declared the winner and will receive a World, Universal, WWE or Women's Championship match of their choice at WrestleMania! Wrestlers from RAW, SmackDown & NXT who currently do not hold their brand's top championship are eligible to participate.
